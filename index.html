Muhammad Ali

Muhammad Ali was an American professional boxer and cultural icon, widely regarded as one of the greatest boxers of all time.

Mike Tyson

Mike Tyson is a former American professional boxer who competed from 1985 to 2005. He is considered one of the greatest heavyweight boxers of all time.
